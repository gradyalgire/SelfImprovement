Immediate Support & Crisis Resources


Crisis & Suicide Support

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (U.S.) — Call or text 988
  • Samaritans (UK & ROI) — Call 116 123
  • Veterans Crisis Line (U.S.) — Call 988 then press 1
  • Find a Helpline — International crisis support

Mental Health Support

  • Open Counseling — Mental health resources by country
  • NAMI — Education, support, and helplines (U.S.)
  • Mind — Mental health support (UK)
  • LGBTQ+ Youth Crisis Support (U.S.) — Call 1-866-488-7386 (24/7) OR Text START to 678678

Addiction & Substance Use

  • SAMHSA Helpline (U.S.) — Call 1-800-662-HELP
  • Alcoholics Anonymous
  • Narcotics Anonymous
  • NHS Addiction Support (UK)

Behavioral & Compulsive Issues

  • NoFap — Pornography addiction recovery
  • SMART Recovery — Addiction & behavioral change
  • Quit — Smoking and nicotine support

If you are in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself, contact local emergency services right now.
